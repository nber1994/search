Neusoft Won the Prize of “2010 Annual Board”from Shanghai Stock Exchange

  July 15th, 2011 From: Global League net

According to the news from CNSN on the 23th, December, the 9th China Enterprises Management Forum charged by Shanghai Stock Exchange and supported by State-owned assets supervision and the organization for economic cooperation and development was held in Shanghai recently. Neusoft won the prize of the 2010 board of directors given by Shanghai Stock Exchange because of its long time standardized management and its outstanding performance in the field of the company management. This is one of the highest honors about management of enterprises given by domestic securities regulatory institutions. It is also an approval from the trade union for the Neusoft in company management.

"The board of directors of Neusoft devotes itself to construction and improvement of corporate governance structure. The management of company and ability of operation gain recognition constantly. We improve the governance structure of the company and create management and business mode by building a international and professional board of director to quicken the pace of international development and provide experience and support for implementation of business expansion and international merger." For the governance structure, the chairman and CEO Liu Jiren has a deep feeling.

As a leading company in IT solution and service provider, Neusoft keeps international management and creates the structure of the board of directors. From the establishment, we always have an international board of director. Neusoft asks its external directors for good education, abundant professional experience or technology and experience in multinational companies, international strategies and sight, can support the international development of Neusoft. At the same time, most of the directors are experts in IT, management, and finance. External directors take most positions in board of directors to ensure the decisions scientific, objective and reasonable. The international and professional board of directors promotes internationalization of Neusoft effectively and provided valuable experience and support for expansion of overseas business and international merger.

On the other hand, Neusoft improves its corporate governance constantly and strengthen capital innovation. The company confirmed its decision authority and process of major issues to ensure the decisions scientific and reasonable by setting rules of debate, access rules and other rules. At the same times, the management and operation gain recognition constantly. In 2007 and 2010, Neusoft acquired “The best board of directors” two times given by “Board of Directors”. So far, Neusoft is component stocks of “Corporate governance index” in Shanghai Exchange House. Neusoft is the only software company in the top 20 of "2010 China Excellent Listed Companies Legal Risk Management".

Shanghai Exchange House evaluated Neusoft that "The board of directors of Neusoft know clearly the outstanding corporate governance is the foundation of operation of listed companies. The company devotes itself to construction and 完善 of corporate governance structure and pays attention to the internationalization of the structure of the board of directors, improve operation of international development and ensure the decisions made by director scientific, practical and forward-looking."

The chairman and CEO of Neusoft, Liu Jiren said, "The prize Neusoft got is an approval at shareholders’ rights, independence of the board of directors, standard operation, credit construction, transparency and other sides. In the future, we will still devote to build high quality governance platform."

"The 9th China Company Governance Forum" was held by Shanghai Exchange House. It is an important forum held for 20 years establishment of Chinese capital market. The theme was looking back and summarizing history and experience of listed companies’ governance, discussing the direction and route of perfecting the company governance. It has important significance. Besides "2010 Annual Board of Directors", there are another two prize "2010 Information Disclosure" and "2010 Excellent Independent Director".
