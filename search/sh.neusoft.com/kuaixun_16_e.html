The “Health Cloud”Covering 310 Thousand People in Dujiangyan

  Febuary 22rd, 2012 From: Sichuan online

The use of the cloud computing has come true. So far the“Health Cloud Computing Plan”of the Neusoft has its primary scale, covering about 310 thousand people. Lu Zhaoxia claimed that the healthy city of Dujiangyan depended on the program health harbor. It means that the Internet terminals have been installed in 200 villages and communities. Citizens can measure their blood pressure, weight, heartbeat and so on through the terminals. And then the results will be saved in the health files on the Internet to realize continual and dynamic management.

"When citizens get ill, they do not need to go a long distance for professional diagnosis."
