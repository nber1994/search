Neusoft Hangzhou

Neusoft Hangzhou is a branch institution of Neusoft in Hangzhou and presents Neusoft to finish all the businesses in Zhejiang. So far there are more than 100 employees and there is a Hangzhou R & D center which has 70 employees. They take charge of the 2nd development and after-sales service. There are 12 technical support engineers and 18 salesmen and saleswomen.

From the establishment of Neusoft Hangzhou in 1996, we have a history of 10 years in Zhejiang, take charge in many large projects and accumulate many clients such as Zhejiang Public Security Department, the Provincial Tax Bureau, Provincial Labor Bureau, Provincial Office of Land and Resource, Provincial Department of Finance Provincial Mobile Company, Provincial Electric Power Company, ICBC(Industrial and Commercial Bank of China) Hangzhou branch, Zhejiang China Tobacco Industrial Company, Hangzhou customs, Hangzhou Labor Bureau, Ningbo traffic police station, Zhoushan bulk stock trade center and so on, making contribution to informatization development of Zhejiang. Neusoft Hangzhou has been listed in excellent company in Hangzhou for many times. In 2010, we became director company of Hangzhou Computer Information and Internet Security Council, the partner of China Mobile Zhejiang Company and gained the honorable mention of informatization application in China mobile industry.

Neusoft Hangzhou belongs to Neusoft EOC. The headquarter of EOC is located in Shanghai and it is a “virtual headquarter” set by Neusoft in east China. It takes charge in all business in the east China. It has Shanghai Neusoft Era Digital Technology Co., Ltd., Shanghai Neusoft System Integration Technology Co., Ltd., Neusoft Hangzhou, Neusoft Nanjing and Neusoft Hefei.

Neusoft devotes itself to become a company which is respected by the society, clients, shareholders and employees. Its service system covers the nation to ensure most users can use local service provided by Neusoft. Besides, Neusoft has 800-8908000 free hotline. It can response users in time.
