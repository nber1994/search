Healthy Community Built by Shimao Travel and Neusoft Xikang

August, 8th, 2012 From: Souhu Focus Net

August, 6th, 2012, the signing ceremony of cooperation of Shimao Yulong and Neusoft in advanced health management system was held in hot spring model area at Yulong Harbor. Liu Saifei, the director of Shimao Travel, and professor Lu Zhaoxia, the chairman and CEO of Neusoft Xikang signed the agreement of Shimao Health Community imported in Xikang Health Management Sytem. The media, journalists and owners saw this moment together. It means that Shimao Yulong Harbor has entered a “healthy medical and life”period.

The “Health Cloud” of Neusoft and Xikang product, Xikang walking watch and Xikang health machine firstly appeared in Dalian. As economy develops, people demand more for their life and the core is the health. But how to achieve the continual monitor and management of individual health information is known by very little people.

Liu Saifei, the executive director, president of Travel real estate and Lu Zhaoxia, the senior vice president, chairman and CEO of Neusoft Xikang signed the agreement

We aim to solve the sustainable development of health. We can monitor the vital signs by give owners Xikang watch to wear and record the health condition of owners through the Xikang health machine to give advice in diet and sport. For the owners who have to go to hospital, Neusoft Xikang shows a professional medical treatment and makes an appointment nationwide for the owners. The purpose that Shimao Travel makes an agreement with Neusoft Xikang is to lead the advanced health management into Shimao Travel national project and construct healthy community. Shimao Dalian Yulong Harbor will become the first project.

As a masterpiece of Shimao Travel real estate, Shimao Yulong Harbor is estimate 20 square kilometers large and in the future there will be a 500 thousand square meters shopping mall, 30 thousand square meters indoor theme park, hot spring hotel, sport park and so on. At the same time, Yulong Harbor will also import medical, education and other facilities to make Shimao Yulong Harbor a tourist resort and a garden city on the sea.

Professor Lu Zhaoxia, the senior vice president, chairman and CEO of Neusoft Xikang said, the cooperation this time brought the advanced health management products and services to community and family and made more people take advantage of cloud computing, content networking and other leading technologies. It is very significant.

Liu Saifei, the president of Shimao Travel real estate said: According to the urban development orientation and trend, combined with the planning idea of Shimao Yulong Harbor project, as the enterance of partners such as Neusoft, the 20 square kilometers sea city will show to us. In August, the only creative fold court products will be launched. Shimao Yulong Harbor will become a landmarks and benchmarking in golden Bo Sea coast, Jinzhou new region and Dalian.
