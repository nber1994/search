Neusoft Was Listed Again among IAOP "2012 Top 100 Global Outsourcing"

  July, 19th , 2012 From: Feixiang Net

On July, 18th ,2012, Neusoft Co., Ltd. Shengyang, Chin announced that Neusoft had been listed 6 times in "Top 100 Global Outsourcing" and become a leading company from China this year.

Besides the main list, Neusoft was also mentioned in many sub-lists, including Top 5 Outsourcing Service Enterprises in Auto Industry, Top 10 Enterprises in Medical Industry, Top 20 Enterprises in Information/Communication Service, Top 20 Enterprises in Research Service, Top 20 in Industry Custom Service, Top 10 in Chinese Market, Top 10 in Japanese Market.

"2012 Top 100 Global Outsourcing" is chose from worldwide outsourcing service enterprises by IAOP. This selection is based on the audit an independent panel organized by IAOP. It adopts strict review mechanism and processes. So the "Top 100 Global Outsourcing" is an important reference for the big companies to cooperate with outsourcing enterprises and becomes an important standard in the field of outsourcing service.

The president of IAOP, Michael Corbett said, "In the world economy today, for the outsourcing terminal users, it is more and more important for them to distinguish and choose suitable companies to meet their outsourcing needs. The lists of Top 100 Global Outsourcing and the Global Best Outsourcing Consultancy Institutions are important reference for the companies that are looking for new partners and expanding their cooperation relationship in the field of outsourcing."
