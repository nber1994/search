The 3.1 Billion RMB Telematics Letter of Intent with Chery

  March 23th, 2012 08:49 From: Huicong automobile net

On March 19th, Neusoft announced that it had signed a letter of intent with Chery Quantum Auto Co (CQAC). From the date of signing to 2019, It is estimated that our income would have 3.1 billion RMB after selling our in-car entertainment information system to CQAC. And at the same time we will spend 2, 9 billion RMB after buying related hardware and platform from ALPINE Electronics (China) Co., Ltd.

At the same time, Neusoft said this letter of intent was not a contract, and might not be carried out. In addition, sales amount will be affected by sales of the cars.

It is said that this is the biggest letter of intent so far in telematics. Also, it brought chances from various fields to telematics. We also can find some problems from weibo. The first is low profit margin of hardware. The second is low profit of integration and distribution without independent intellectual property rights. The third is the time span is long. Though the total amount is huge, the average profit can be low.

Related information:

As a software and service provider in automobile electronic field, Neusoft has 20 years experience and cooperate with ALPINE, Harman and other manufactures. Neusoft has accumulated abundant experience and technologies and has built a electronic software development teams in China, Germany, Japan, more than 2,000 employees in total. We are in a ascendancy.

ALPINE is a leading manufacture in high quality automotive multimedia and has a good and stable strategic partnership with Neusoft. In the past cooperation, Neusoft took advantages of software development to provide ALPINE software development and outsourcing service in the fields such as car audio, navigation.

This time, the project takes a different business model. It is meaningful, including:

1. As the quick development of Chinese auto industry, China has become the biggest auto market. No matter international manufactures or local manufactures focus more and more on the products research aimed to Chinese market, especially the automobile electronic components. Neusoft relies on its abundant experience in technology development in the international market and its advantage that Neusoft has a profound understanding of Chinese market. We get new chance by innovation of technology and application. This project with Chery is the result of business growth motivated by technology advantages. Neusoft will strengthen the connection between company development and client business scale with new business model and new thought to achieve sustainable development and transformation of Neusoft’s auto electronic business.

2. This time we take different business model. Not only can we combine the advantages of both sides in domestic and international market, software and hardware, but also Neusoft can help ALPINE expand the market in China. By the innovative cooperation model, we can deepen strategic cooperation in products research, technology research and sales to create more value.

According to the budget, under the process of the letter of intent, in the cycle of the production of Chery Quantum Auto, Neusoft will purchase related hardware and platform from ALPINE China. The number of purchase depends on the production of the auto. According to the expected production, from the date the letter of intent was signed to 2019, it is estimated that the additional cost will be about 2.9 billion yuan.
