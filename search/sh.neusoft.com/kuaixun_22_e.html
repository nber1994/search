Neusoft Inspection System for Abnormal Flow Won China Patent Excellent Award

  November 26th, 2011 From: Way to Investment

Recently, the State Intellectual Property Office (SIPO) published the result of the 13th Chinese patent award (http://www.sipo.gov.cn/tz/pingxuan/201111/t20111104_627867.html ). There are 697 patents selected by 19 Central Department, 46 regional intellectual property bureaus, 39 guilds, 102 academicians of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chinese Academy of Engineering. The number reached a highest point. By review of China's patent award review committee, State Intellectual Property Office and The World Intellectual Property Organization, there were 15 gold prizes of Chinese patent, 15 design gold prize, and 177 honorable mentions for Chinese patent, 35 design honorable mentions. The NetEye of Neusoft “a kind of way and system that can monitor the abnormal flow” won a honorable mention for Chinese patent.

This patent technology has been used in Neusoft NetEye Network Traffic Analysis and Response System (NTARS). NTARS is a network analysis system faced to the large network of government, enterprises, operators, and other fields. It helps users to know the constitute, flow, agreement distribution and service conditions by supporting the agreement of main equipment and get flow analysis agreement bags which go through the network equipment. NTARS solve the “4W” for users (Who, When, What and Where).

Different from traditional flow analysis software based on SNMP, mirror image or beam split, NTARS analyses the flow by getting configuration of flow output at related routers or switches. It not only can solve the distribution and constitute problems of the flow, but also can judge and trace the network fault caused by DDoS attack, Bot-Net, worms outbreak, internal malicious users and so on to simplify the complicated flow problems and achieve long-term analysis and monitor.

The excellent patent projects in the selection were chose from thousands of hundreds patents. They have high technical level, low energy consumption, and good efficiency. They are outstanding examples of quickening independent innovation and intellectual property industrialization.
