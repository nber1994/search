    Introduction

    Neusoft Shanghai R&D center locates at Zhangjiang High-Tech Park. It consists of three main teams to provide such services as consulting, system integration and software development, with a staff team that most of whom are excellent graduates from top universities as Tsinghua University and Zhejiang University. In 2012, the R&D center will be moved to Zizhu Park in Minhang district. The overall size and the working environment will be further enhanced to have the capacity to accommodate more than 3,000 employees.

    Technical direction

    As cable network is getting mature and Wi-Fi is rising, traditional software companies are facing new opportunities and more development space. Neusoft Shanghai R&D center will mobilize the client database accumulated by Neusoft Group over the past 20 years and fully utilize mobile Internet value chain and ecosystem in accordance to group`s B2B2C strategy, to provide innovative applications for end users. They all base on the customers accumulated for more than 20 years. Our main business areas will cover health and medicine, new media, mobile pay, enterprises social network, education, entertainment and smart city.

    For internet applications which face a large number of end users, the technologies of front-end user interaction design, back-end large-scale concurrent processing and the save of abundant data are big challenges. On the one hand, Shanghai R&D center will promote and perfect the framework of cloud calculation. On the other hand, as the gain of projects, Shanghai R&D center will accumulate the ability to develop intelligent terminals, improve the creative ability, especially to offer more brilliant experience for industry application by using multimedia and virtual reality techniques.


    Strategy for development and long-range planning

    In the future 5 years, Shanghai R&D center aims to attract more IT talents, to enhance the R&D ability, to enlarge the total scale, and to provide our customers with overall services from analysis to development, even to operation. At the same time, Shanghai R&D center will explore more users’ potential demands and combine the direction of leading technology and foreseeing incubation projects to bring the creativity of technology and application to our customers. We will also work with our strategic partners for new collaboration ways, and to build up innovative win-win business models.
